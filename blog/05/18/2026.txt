First Day of Summer?? - May 18, 2026

Today I learned that geese mate for life. I was sitting inside when a flock of 
geese came close by my window. The goslings grouped around their mother and proudly 
snacked on the front lawn as I watched. My landlord joked about a goose named Lonely
Larry who often walked through the park all by himself since he failed to find a partner.
She talked about a recent accident in which someone ran over a goose and how her and 
her neighbors noticed one of the male geese seemed distraught over the loss. I really
like animals so it made me feel sad thinking about it. It is quite beautiful how some
animals can experience such emotional complexity.
